About Me

Hello! I am an undergraduate senior at MIT (Class of 2018). I study Computer Science & Math. I was born in and grew up in Pensacola, FL.

I am taking this class to learn how to fabricate at every scale and to understand what problems fabrication is facing. Prior experience with fabrication is minimal. ☺ I care about interfaces & design. I want to learn more about why and how we use the things in our lives.
